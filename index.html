Why Recreactiv' ?

Since 2019, I gave been giving scolar support for students in need. I really enjoyed seeing them progressing and bring home better grades. This is how the idea of creating a website to get in touch students and tutors came out. Recreactiv' is a mix between fun and learning, as the lessons are given from students to students.

How does it work?

Tutors wanting to share their knowledge with students in difficulty offer their time (minimum 1 hour per week) to help them. Students can discuss on the dedicated platform and meet in a safe place, among those offered on the application (libraries, cafes, dedicated rooms, etc.). According to where you live, you can choose a place and a teacher below !
